What are the visas that I can apply for permanent residency?

Australia has different visas leading to permanent residency available for migrants. These visas are often referred to as skilled-migration visas (Genera Skilled Migration). Here are some of the well-known visas:

  1. Skilled Independent Visa – Subclass 189 *Points-stream

  2. Skilled Nominated Visa – Subclass 190

  3. Skilled Regional Sponsored Visa – Subclass 489 (State or Family Sponsorship Streams Available)

Occupations that are eligible for these visas are listed out on the Combined Skilled Occupation List on the Home Affairs Website.
